We understand the challenge and many of our customers had similar concerns as you. To address your concern, we suggest two different options for using our products.

1) You can adopt a crawl-walk-run approach to use our products: your staff can start using the product 15 min to 45 min a day, and then as you see the ROI, you can have your staff allocate more time without disrupting your current work process and resource.

2) For those who do not have any staff to oversee our products, we offer a turnkey solution: we have a monitoring center that can onboard your members to our platform, monitor their physiological readings, interact with your members, and alert any clinical events that need physicians' attention. For more information, please contact info@ormahealth.com

Patients do not need to be on Medicare to use our products, but they may not be covered by the Remote Patient Monitoring procedure codes (CPT 99453, 454, 457, 458). To maximize the ROI of our platform, we recommend prioritizing Medicare patients first.m

As long as patients are under Medicare, patients do not need to pay for the devices or to get monitored. However, patients may be subject to copay if they do not have Medi-Gap insurance (supplemental plan), where more than 80% of Medicare patients have supplemental plans.
